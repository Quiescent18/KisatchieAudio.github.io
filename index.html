About Me

Hey there! Thanks for stopping by. I've been a musician for most of my life, and that passion naturally led me down the path of audio engineering/production. What started as a personal mission to make better sounding demos for my own projects, quickly turned into something more.


As my skills grew, fellow musicians began reaching out for help with their own recordings. Today, I offer professional recording, mixing, and mastering services tailored to artists who care about capturing the emotion, energy, and clarity their music deserves.

Every artist is unique. Why sound like everyone else?

Whether you're looking to polish a single, shape the sonic identity of an EP, or bring cohesion to a full-length album, I'm here to help make your sound everything it can be. I offer a hybrid setup that utilizes outboard gear to combat the digital sterility that often plagues home recordings.


Keep scrolling to hear some samples of my work and feel free to reach out if you'd like to work together.
